Julian Lee-Sursin

Hi! I'm Julian. I do philosophy and cognitive science (mostly). I'm currently a visiting scholar at Johns Hopkins, finishing an MS in cognitive science at the ENS, and just completed an MPhil at La Sorbonne.

My research focuses on explanation, language, and meaning—including how non-linguistic things like fashion can communicate. I also think about AI and computational neuroscience.
